    Looking for an activity which is fun, challenging and will test your head for heights

    Then a high level ropes course is for you, with a variety of elements to challenge you in different ways such as, Jacobs Ladder, Monkey Swings, Criss-cross, Indian Rope Bridge, Balance Beam, Postmans Walk, Grape Vine, Leap of Faith and the 90 Meter Zip Wire.

    Ropes courses invite you to surmount obstacles and physical puzzles at a range of heights above the forest floor. High beams, rope bridges, zip wires and climbing walls are great fun and can promote personal growth and teambuilding.

    Challenge your balance and co-ordination on our adrenaline-fuelled treetop ropes courses. . You will climb 50 feet up into the woodland canopy. And then weave through the trees on our specially designed trail. Test your WhooHoo!-ometer ratings on a range of intricately constructed swings, bridges, wooden walkways and zip wires.

    This redefines living for the moment. Surf the treetops, experience the views and feel the adrenaline rush. When you come back down to earth you will be on a natural high. Your journey is guaranteed to be a challenge, but you will always be linked up to a harness for safety, and will receive a full safety briefing before you leave the ground. The continuous safety system allows participants to move between obstacles without the need to unclip.
